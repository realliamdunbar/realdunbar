Information

Froylan Fahlan Aditya, known online as Liam Dunbar, is an 18-year-old from Indonesia. He has a calm but introspective presence. He speaks Indonesian and English with a preference for a UK accent, and he’s currently learning German. His heart is drawn to Austria and Germany — places he dreams of living in someday.


Fahlan Aditya is a quiet soul with many storms inside. He grew up being told to silence his emotions, but deep down he feels everything — maybe too much. His past carved wounds that never fully healed. He calls himself “a boy made of water and soil,” fragile yet undeniably real.


Though life has been cruel, Fahlan has a beauty in him that doesn’t fade: his empathy. He sees pain in others even when no one else notices. He protects the broken, the lonely, the forgotten. That’s how his heart works: raw, human, protective.
