Fahlan Aditya is a quiet soul who carries many storms inside.He grew up being told to silence his emotions, but deep down he feels everything - maybe too much. His past carved wounds that never fully healed, leaving him torn between hating and yearning for connection. Wounds that never heal

Social Anxiety

noun

An anxiety disorder that is characterized by excessive fear of social situations. Usually, it is a fear of being judged or negatively evaluated by others.

Depression

noun

A mental illness in which a person feels very unhappy and anxious for long periods of time and cannot live a normal life during those periods.

Intermittent Explosive Disorder (IED)

noun

A mental health condition marked by sudden, intense outbursts of anger or violence that are disproportionate to the situation, often followed by regret or remorse.

Complex PTSD (C-PTSD)

noun

A condition that can develop after chronic trauma and cause difficulties with relationships and emotion regulation.

Autism Spectrum Disorder

noun

A developmental condition that affects how a person thinks, feels, communicates, and interacts with others. It often includes challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, and sensitivity to sensory input.

Borderline Personality Traits (BPD)

noun

A pattern of emotional instability, impulsive behavior, distorted self-image, and unstable relationships. People with BPD traits may struggle with fear of abandonment and intense mood swings.

Executive Dysfunction

noun

A disruption in the brain's ability to manage tasks, focus, plan ahead, control impulses, or follow through on goals. It often makes everyday functioning difficult and exhausting.

Dissociative Symptoms

noun

Mental experiences of disconnection or detachment from reality, self, thoughts, or surroundings. It can feel like being on "autopilot," feeling unreal, or watching yourself from outside your body.

Chronic Loneliness and Alienation

noun

A long-term, painful feeling of being isolated, disconnected, or different from others, even when not physically alone. It's often linked to feeling unseen, misunderstood, or unwanted.
